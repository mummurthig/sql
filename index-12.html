FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

How long does a typical project take?

Most requests are turned around within 48 hours, though more complex tasks like full landing pages or branded systems can take 3–5 days. You’ll always get a clear timeline upfront.

What does the collaboration process look like?

We start with a kickoff call to align on goals, then move into structured sprints with weekly reviews. You’ll always know what’s shipping next and when.

Who will be my main point of contact?

A dedicated project lead will own day-to-day communication, with direct access to our designers, developers, and strategists whenever needed.

How do you handle revisions and feedback?

Every milestone includes structured review rounds. Feedback flows through a single shared channel so nothing slips, and revisions are always scoped before we ship.

What is included in the final delivery?

You receive production-ready files, a documented design system, source code with clear handoff notes, and a 30-day post-launch support window.

Can we scale the project as we grow?

Absolutely. Every system we build is structured for expansion, with a clear upgrade path from initial launch to long-term partnership.

What technologies and frameworks do you use?

Figma for design, Next.js and React for production, Webflow and Framer for marketing sites — we pick the stack that best fits your team and scale.

How do I initiate a new project?

Send us a brief through the contact form and we’ll schedule a discovery call within two business days to align on scope, timeline, and budget.
