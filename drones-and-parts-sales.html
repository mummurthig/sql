Drones & Parts Sales

Premium Components & Custom FPV Solutions

Whether you are building your first freestyle quad or need a heavy-lift cinematic rig for a professional shoot, we provide the highest quality components sourced directly from top manufacturers.

We also offer custom drone building services. Our expert technicians will assemble, solder, tune, and test fly your rig to perfection before handing it over.

What We Supply

From raw carbon fiber frames to advanced digital video systems, we stock reliable parts for every type of pilot.

Custom Built Drones

Don't want to build it yourself? Tell us your requirements (freestyle, cinematic, long-range, or industrial) and we will construct a custom BNF (Bind-and-Fly) drone tuned specifically for your needs.

Frames & Motors

Durable carbon fiber frames for 3-inch cinewhoops up to 10-inch macro quads. High-efficiency brushless motors from premium brands like T-Motor, Xing, and BrotherHobby.

Electronics (FC & ESC)

Reliable flight controllers and electronic speed controllers. We stock F4 and F7 stacks capable of handling high-amperage draw for aggressive flying and heavy payloads.

Video Systems (VTX & Cameras)

Digital and analog video transmission systems. We carry DJI O3 Air Units, Caddx Vista/Walksnail kits, TBS Unify Pro, and high-quality FPV cameras from Runcam and Foxeer.

We create cinematic FPV experiences, professional aerial media, and industrial drone solutions with precision, innovation, and unmatched flight performance across India.
