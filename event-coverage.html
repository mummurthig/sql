Our Capabilities

What Sets Our Coverage Apart

Purpose-built FPV systems designed for the demands of live events—speed, adaptability, and cinematic quality in one package.

  • Live-Event Real-Time Monitoring

    Our pilots use dedicated FPV goggles for first-person precision flying, ensuring every shot is intentionally composed rather than left to chance automation.

  • Certified Pilots + Full Safety Protocol

    Every event deployment includes a DGCA-certified remote pilot, spotter crew, and a comprehensive pre-event safety brief aligned with the venue's requirements.

  • 4K Stabilized Output with Same-Day Teaser

    We can deliver a short teaser highlight (30–60 sec) within hours of your event ending—perfect for social media posting while the buzz is still live.

  • Multi-Location & Multi-Day Events

    We operate seamlessly across multi-venue setups. Our fleet of drones ensures continuous coverage with zero downtime between battery changes.

  • Broadcast & Social-Ready Deliverables

    We deliver final footage in multiple formats—4K for broadcast/OTT, 1080p for web, and vertical 9:16 crop for Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts.
