3D Point Clouds

Drone Surveying vs. Traditional Methods

Traditional land surveying using Total Stations is slow, labor-intensive, and limits data to sparse points. Drone photogrammetry revolutionizes this process.

Fraction of the Time

What takes a ground crew a week to survey, our RTK drones can map in a single day, dramatically accelerating your project timeline.

Enhanced Safety

Survey hazardous, steep, or inaccessible terrain (like active mines or unstable slopes) without putting personnel at risk.

Millions of Data Points

Instead of a few hundred points collected manually, drones capture millions of points, providing a continuous, high-density reality capture.

Methodology

Our End-to-End Workflow

We follow a strict, standardized operational procedure to guarantee survey-grade absolute accuracy and reliable deliverables.

01

Project Planning

Airspace checks, parameter definition, and automated flight path generation based on required GSD.

02

GCP Placement

Laying and recording Ground Control Points using DGPS/RTK rovers for absolute spatial accuracy.

03

Data Acquisition

Autonomous drone flights capturing high-overlap nadir/oblique imagery across the site.

04

Photogrammetry

Processing thousands of images into cohesive orthomosaics and 3D models using advanced software.

05

Quality & Delivery

Accuracy verification against checkpoints and delivery of CAD/GIS ready file formats.
