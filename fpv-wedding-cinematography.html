The Process

How We Bring Your Vision to Life

From the initial consultation to the final film delivery, every step is crafted to ensure a seamless, stress-free experience on your special day.

01
Discovery Consultation

We begin with a detailed conversation about your wedding style, venue, timeline, and the key moments you want captured. We scout your venue virtually and prepare a customized shot list tailored to your love story.

02
Pre-Event Site Reconnaissance

Our pilots visit the venue in advance to map flight corridors, identify ideal camera angles, check for interference, and coordinate with venue management to ensure we are fully ready on your big day.

03
Day-of Cinematic Coverage

Our certified pilot and cinematographer team arrives early to set up. We operate silently and unobtrusively throughout the day—capturing your Mehendi, Sangeet, Baraat, ceremony, and reception.

04
Professional Post-Production

Our editing team applies color grading, gyro stabilization, and cinematic sound design to transform raw footage into a breathtaking film. Turnaround is typically 3–5 weeks after your event.

05
Delivery & Archiving

Receive your final film via private download link in 4K resolution. We also provide a highlight reel optimized for Instagram/YouTube. All raw footage is archived securely for 6 months.

FAQs

Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know before booking our FPV wedding cinematography service.

We use specialized Cinewhoop FPV drones with fully enclosed propeller guards that prevent any contact with people or surfaces. These drones are specifically engineered for indoor proximity flying and are the industry standard for indoor cinematography worldwide. Our pilots are certified and trained to operate in crowded environments safely.
We handle all necessary regulatory paperwork on your behalf. Our pilots hold valid DGCA Remote Pilot Certificates. For outdoor flying, we assess airspace classifications. For indoor flying, no DGCA permissions are required. We will coordinate with your venue directly to secure all necessary approvals well before your wedding day.
We strongly recommend booking at least 2–3 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (November to February). Popular dates fill up very quickly. A 50% advance payment secures your date. We do sometimes accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability, so feel free to reach out.
Standard delivery is 3–5 weeks after your event. This includes footage review, stabilization, color grading, music licensing, and final export. If you need a social media teaser delivered faster (within 1 week), we can prioritize that at no extra cost for Premium and Elite packages.
