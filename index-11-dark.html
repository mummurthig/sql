Crafted with intention,
built to endure.

Founded in 2019, Orisa Motion is an independent creative studio specialising in branded film, motion design, and visual effects. We work with challenger brands, cultural institutions, and Fortune 500 companies to craft stories that move people — literally.

How we work

A process built for bold work.

From first brief to final frame, our four-stage methodology keeps every project focused, collaborative, and on time.

Learn about our approach
  1. 01

    Discovery & Strategy

    We open with a deep-dive workshop — audience mapping, competitive analysis, and defining the single feeling the film must leave behind.

    Brief review Audience mapping Creative brief
  2. 02

    Concept & Storyboard

    Three visual concepts, each with a distinct tonal direction, moodboard, shot list, and animatic. You choose the direction; we refine it.

    Moodboard Storyboard Animatic
  3. 03

    Production & VFX

    Shoot days, CG renders, motion design, and editorial — all managed under one roof with daily review links and transparent revision tracking.

    Direction VFX Editorial
  4. 04

    Delivery & Launch

    Platform-optimised exports, DCP packages, and a post-launch 30-day support window. We're still on call after the credits roll.

    QC & Mastering Multi-platform export Support
