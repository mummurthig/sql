The Chennai FPV Standard

We don't just assemble parts. We engineer reliable aerial vehicles built to withstand the rigors of professional use.

Flawless Soldering

Medical-grade solder joints. No cold joints, no bridging. We guarantee reliable electrical connections that won't fail mid-flight.

Clean Wire Routing

Meticulous cable management. Wires are cut to exact lengths, twisted for EMF reduction, and secured away from props.

Conformal Coating

Complete waterproofing of all sensitive electronics. Fly through fog, light rain, or morning dew with absolute confidence.

Betaflight Blackbox Tuning

Propwash elimination and crisp stick response. We use Blackbox logging to tune PID and filter settings specifically for your frame.

How It Works

01

Consultation & Component Selection

We discuss your goals—freestyle bashing, indoor real estate tours, or carrying a RED Komodo. We then curate a tailored parts list matching your budget and requirements.

02

Expert Assembly

Our technicians begin the build. We ensure proper soft-mounting of the gyro, secure VTX placement for cooling, and apply thread-locker to all structural bolts.

03

Firmware & Tuning

We flash the latest stable Betaflight/INAV firmware, configure your OSD, bind your receiver, and set up your preferred switch layout on your radio transmitter.

04

Test Flight & Delivery

No drone leaves our shop without an aggressive line-of-sight test flight. We verify video range, motor temperatures, and failsafe behavior before shipping it to you.
