OUR PROCESS

How we shape every project, from blueprint to build.

A disciplined five-phase methodology where every drawing, model, and decision earns its place. Slow when it matters, swift when it counts.

  • 12-24w AVG TIMELINE
  • 05 PHASES
01
Week 1-2

Discovery

Site analysis, brief workshops, programmatic studies, and constraint mapping. We listen first, then sketch.

DELIVERABLES

  • +Site Survey
  • +Program Brief
  • +Feasibility Memo
02
Week 3-5

Concept

Massing studies, material palettes, and design narrative. We test ideas in physical models and computational forms.

DELIVERABLES

  • +Concept Decks
  • +Massing Models
  • +Mood Boards
03
Week 6-10

Schematic

Plans, sections, and elevations resolved. Structural and MEP coordination begins. The building gains its bones.

  • +3D Renderings

Documentation
